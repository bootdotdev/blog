## Open positions

### Video Editor/Animator - Remote - Full Time

We're looking for an in-house video editor and animator to help us produce more and better programming explainer videos and walkthroughs for our [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC9HOZ53gnHP3f_b-wixS74g).

**What you'll do:**

- Edit and animate explainer videos for our courses
- Edit and animate advertisements and B-roll content for marketing purposes
- Be involved and help with the creative process of planning and executing videos
- Plan and execute a strategy to get our organic content in front of more folks on social

**What you'll need to succeed:**

- Experience creating and editing engaging videos
- Ability to design custom 2D animations (after effects)
- Basic social media prowess (know how to craft a good visual hook)
- Great audio/visual taste
- Proficiency in programming basics (Python, Git, SQL, etc)
- No on-paper experience required, but you _will_ need a banger portfolio to be considered

**Compensation:**

- (Depends on experience and location):
  - Tier 1: ~$75,000 + stock options (USA, etc) Health insurance if in the US
  - Tier 2: ~$55,000 + stock options (Canada, UK, Australia, etc)
  - Tier 3: ~$35,000 + stock options (India, LATAM, etc)
  - _Note: Options are 4-year vesting with a 1-year cliff_

**To apply:**

Send an email to Lane at Boot.dev with:

- Your resume
- Links to the best 3 videos that you've edited and animated
- A personal note about why you're interested in working with us
